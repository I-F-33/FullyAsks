About Us

Welcome to the Titan Challenge project, Our team consists of people who really care about technology, education, and working together.

Together, we want to make a cool way for people to get familiar with CSUF, that shows how awesome Cal State Fullerton is with its history, campus, and great education.

The story behind Titan Challenge stemmed from a desire to address the common challenge faced by new students at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF): how to quickly and effectively familiarize themselves with the school's history, facilities, and resources related to their major.

The mission of Titan Challenge is to provide an engaging and informative platform for new students at California State University, Fullerton (CSUF)

Our mission is to empower students to feel confident and connected to their university community from the moment they arrive on campus.
